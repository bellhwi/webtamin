Q. What should I prepare for development requests? A. If you have any articles, images, videos, or logos you want to use on the website, please prepare them.
Q. How long does it take to make the website? A. It takes about 30 days. It can be shortened or extended depending on the size of the project.
Q. How is the production process? A. Fill out the application - Sign the contract - Q&A and discussion about the business - Start development - Deliver 1st draft - Get 1st feedback - Deliver 2nd draft- Get 2nd feedback - Complete website - Perform ongoing maintenance
Q. When and how can I pay for the fee? A. One-time: We will charge you 30% of the total fee for the down payment. Once you confirm the final result, we charge you the balance which is 70% of the total fee. Available payment methods are Zelle, PayPal, and check.
Subscription: We will charge an initial $150 after signing up the contract. From the second month, the same amount will be charged on the 1st of every month.
Q. How do I register my domain and own my personal email? A. We create an account on the domain provider and send it to you. We will then help you with the registration process.
Q. Is it possible to redesign the existing website? A. We will proceed the same way as creating a new website. The production fee and the production period are the same.
Q. Who will have intellectual property rights for code and design after the production of the website? A. It is owned by Webtamin. Source code and assets used for development are not provided. You own domain and contents.
