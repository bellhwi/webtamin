FAQ

What makes hand-coded websites better than those built with page builders?

Hand-coded websites have less bloated code, resulting in faster loading speeds, better performance, and enhanced customer satisfaction. They are also more customizable and SEO-friendly.

How does on-page SEO help my website rank higher on Google?

On-page SEO involves optimizing individual web pages to rank higher and earn more relevant traffic in search engines. It includes optimizing content, HTML code, and internal links, which makes your site more attractive to search engines.

How long does it take to get leads with digital marketing?

SEO (Search Engine Optimization): Typically 3 to 6 months. SEO improves your website's organic search rankings and takes time to see results due to the gradual build-up of authority and relevance.

PPC (Pay-Per-Click) Advertising: Usually immediate to 1 month. Google Ads PPC campaigns can start generating leads as soon as they are launched, though optimization for best results may take a few weeks.

How much does your digital marketing services cost?

The cost of digital marketing services depends on your specific needs and goals. For a tailored recommendation and the most effective plan, consult with us, and we'll provide a customized solution to fit your requirements.

What is included in your content writing service?

Our content writing service includes creating keyword-optimized content that accurately represents your brand and engages your audience. This helps your website attract and convert visitors more effectively.

Will my website be mobile-friendly?

Yes, we build responsive websites that look great on all devices, including desktops, tablets, and smartphones. This ensures you won't lose leads from any device.

Why should I invest in regular blog posting?

Regular blog posting helps establish your authority in your industry, keeps your website content fresh, and enhances your website’s SEO. It engages your audience and provides valuable information that can lead to higher conversion rates.

What are local citations and why are they important?

Local citations are online mentions of your business that include your name, address, and phone number. They improve your visibility in local search results, driving more foot traffic to your physical location.

What can I expect from your email marketing service?

Our email marketing service includes designing and implementing campaigns to keep your customers informed and engaged. We create newsletters and promotional emails to maintain a direct line of communication with your audience, driving repeat business and customer loyalty.

How does social media management help my business?

Social media management involves creating and scheduling posts that engage your audience and promote your brand. It helps you stay connected with your customers and increases your brand’s visibility on platforms like Facebook and Instagram.

How do you ensure my website stands out in search results?

We use a combination of on-page SEO, content writing, local citations, and PPC campaigns to improve your website’s search engine ranking. Our comprehensive approach ensures your website stands out and attracts more potential customers.

How can Google Ads Pay-per-click campaigns benefit my business?

Google Ads PPC campaigns drive targeted traffic to your landing pages, helping you get more leads instantly. We manage and optimize your campaigns to maximize your ROI and stay within your budget.
