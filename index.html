The hard truth no one tells you
- will a website really benefit your business?

You DON'T need a website

I’ll be honest with you. You don’t necessarily need a website to run your business. I have talked to a lot of successful business owners.

They don’t have a website. Yet they have established good credibility for their customers. Their phones are ringing every day. They are happy with their businesses.

Then what is the point of having a website? It comes down to the three essential things in business. Without a website, you will;

  • lose potential customers online
  • lack of credibility for your business
  • fall behind as your competitors have an online presence

These are core values to run a successful business. You will miss out if you don't have a website. Find more in-depth information in my article - 8 Benefits of a website for small businesses.

About Me

Hello, I am Johnny, a web designer in Sacramento, CA.

In 2019, one of my middle school students said he learned coding in school. I got interested because school never taught me that. I started to develop some simple games and websites as a hobby.

One day, my wife who was my girlfriend at that time, said she needed a portfolio website. I built a website to present her graphic design works. Before long, she got a job with good comments about her site. I learned my web development skills can solve real-world problems.

Since then, I’ve been sharing my programming experience on YouTube to help people learn how to code. After a few years, I started a web design agency in Sacramento to help small business owners build their online presence. Check out what I prioritize in building a website.
