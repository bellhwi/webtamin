About Us

Hello, I am Johnny, a founder of Webtamin in Sacramento, CA.

In 2019, one of my middle school students said he learned coding in school. I got interested because school never taught me that. I started to develop some simple games and websites as a hobby. (Let me know if you are a game person. I’ve got you a hard-core platformer haha)

One day, my wife who was my girlfriend at that time, said she needed a portfolio website. I built a website to present her graphic design works. Before long, she got a job with good comments about her site. I learned my web development skills can solve real-world problems.

Since then, I’ve been sharing my programming experience on YouTube to help people who teach themselves how to code. After a few years, I started a web design company to help small business owners build their online presence. Check out what I prioritize in building a website.
